Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. He is known for hisleadership in advancing Google's flagship products and spearheading innovations in technology. He joined Google in 2004, leading the product management and innovation efforts for a suite ofGoogle's client software products, including Google Chrome and Chrome OS. Pichai's leadership and innovativethinking propelled him through the ranks, and he became Google's CEO in 2015, later taking on the role of CEO ofAlphabet in 2019.

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, is a visionary entrepreneur and engineer known for founding and leading multiple high-impact companies, including Tesla, SpaceX, Neuralink, and The Boring Company. Musk moved to the United States to attend the University of Pennsylvania, where he earned degrees in economics and physics. He initially gained fame and fortune through the sale of his web software company Zip2 and his stake in PayPal.
